16      NO_SENSE(0x00),

22       * error to report (e.g., first, last, most severe) is vendor specific.

28       */
29      NOT_READY(0x2),

32       * condition that may have been caused by a flaw in the medium or an error
33       * in the recorded data. This sense key may also be returned if the device

43      HARDWARE_ERROR(0x4),

50       * prohibits processing the command; d) There was an illegal parameter in
51       * the CDB; or e) There was an illegal parameter in the additional
52       * parameters supplied as data for some commands (e.g., PERSISTENT RESERVE

54       *

78      BLANK_CHECK(0x8),

82      VENDOR_SPECIFIC(0x9),
